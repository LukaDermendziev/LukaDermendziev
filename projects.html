Red Cross Sveti Nikole


THE WAY FROM 100 BOOKS SOLD TO THREE PRICELESS SMILES
The CKA-PHV project was a huge challenge for us, which we accepted with pleasure and without hesitation. Our group chose to work on a project - action in the community, that is, we decided to help a socially - endangered family. The employees of the center for social work and the Red Cross of the municipality of Sveti Nikole pointed out to us the Petkovski family - a single mother with two minor children, who live in a rural area - village. Peshirovo. On 11.04.2022, we visited our target group. During the visit, we talked with the mother, that is, we did research about what they need most.
With tears in his eyes he told us that they didn't have proper beds to sleep on, they don't have a kitchen, so she can't cook food for her children, they don't have food and clothes. After the visit ended we had a short meeting where we decided to provide the family with a bed, kitchen, stove, kitchen utensils (glasses, plates, cutlery), food packages and clothes....
